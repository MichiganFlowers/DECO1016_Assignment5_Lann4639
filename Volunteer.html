As the lifeblood of our organisation volunteers are always welcomed with open arms.

Our volunteers are given the oppurtunity to work in a hands on environment, changing the lives of others. Alongside this service to others our volunteers are given a strong sense of prode and satisfaction.

As a volunteer you will be involved in assisting with:

  • Puppy Raising
  • Corporate Volunteering
  • Events
  • Selling Merchandise
  • Assisting at our Guide Dog Centre
  • Administration

The Caine Court Program

In the Canine Court Companion Program therapy dogs are provided in publicly acessbile areasof the court to assist with lowering stress for victims, vulnerable witnesses, children and victims of domestic and faily violence.

These dogs are specifically trained by the Guide Dogs NSW foundation with current operations occuring in:

  • Manly
  • Gosford
  • Sutherland
  • Burwood
  • Campbelltown
  • Lismore
  • Goulburn
  • Nowra
  • Orange
  • Wagga Wagga
