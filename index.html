Sight Lost Freedom Found

Since 1951 we have vowed to provide the gift of independence to those in need. Through continual persistance in ethical breeding and training methods our guide dogs have given hope to those in need across Australia. This has been achieved through the facilitation of companionship, mobility assistance, advocation for greater acessibility in community infastrucutre and traning to those with visual impariments.

These wonderful gifts of independence are not possible without YOU. The people of generosity who ensure those troubled in sight are still able to experience the beauty of our planet without being left in the dark
