Our Puppies

Bred in an ethical manner our Guide Dogs provide the ultimate gift of sight and more importantly a sense of independence to those not fortunate enough to experience these benefits on a regular basis.

With your generous contribution we here at Guide Dogs NSW can effectively expand our Guide Program by

  • Providing improved training and enrichment equipment
  • Ensuring greater kennel maintinence
  • Cover veterinary costs
  • Employ expert dog training staff

What can I expect to experience as a puppy sponser?

As a sponser you will experience the privilege of closely following the journey taken by your chosen puppy. With your support these puppies can become loved and in return provide the ultimate companionship of being the light of someone's day.
